Salad

An image of a tasty salad

A delicious dish that is easy to make, and low in calories.

Ingredients

  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Mini tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Steps

  1. Pluck the lettuce into palm size pieces and put in a bowl.
  2. Slice the tomatoes in half and place it with the lettuce.
  3. Slice the feta cheese into cubes and place it with the lettuce.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the mix.
  5. Drizzle balsemic vinegar over the mix and serve.
