Bee Hoon Soup

An image of a fishball noodle soup

A delicious dish that is common in Singapore and Malaysia. It is one of my wife's favorite dish.

Ingredients

  • Bee hoon
  • Fishballs
  • Anchovies
  • Fried scallion
  • minced meat

Steps

  1. Make the soup by boiling anchovies in water for 15mins.
  2. Sieve out the anchovies
  3. Put in the beehoon and cook for 10min
  4. Put in the fishball and minced meat
  5. Turn off the fire, put in the scallions and lettuce for decoration
