Spaghetti

An image of a tasty dish of spaghetti

A delicious dish that is easy to make, and this is my favorite.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Prego tomato sauce
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Minced meat

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a pot of boiling water for 10mins. Add a pinch of salt to the water.
  2. Oil a pan with the olive oil on low heat, cook the minced meat.
  3. Once minced meat is cooked, add the tomato sauce and mix.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the tomato sauce mix.
  5. Put some shreded mozerella cheese on the spaghetti and serve.
